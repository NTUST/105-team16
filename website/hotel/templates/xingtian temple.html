Located in Taipei City, Xingtian Temple is one of the most popular temples in Taiwan.
The temple advocates in building a harmonic society from the philosophies of Confucian and Buddhist.
The population of visitors sometimes exceeds more than twenty-thousand people a day.
The reasons for its popularity are due to its beliefs in creating a harmonic society, thus establishing public goods to the communities.
